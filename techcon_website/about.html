Past Speakers

Elon Musk

Elon Musk is the CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, known for his visionary pursuits in space exploration, renewable energy, and electric vehicles. His talks often focus on the future of sustainable energy, Mars colonization, and the integration of technology into everyday life.

Andrew Ng

Andrew Ng is a computer scientist and entrepreneur known for his work on artificial intelligence and machine learning. He co-founded Google Brain and Coursera, and his talks often explore the applications of AI, the future of automation, and the ethical implications of AI technology.

Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg is the COO of Facebook and author of "Lean In." She is an advocate for women in technology and leadership roles. Her talks address leadership strategies, gender equality in the workplace, and the role of technology in driving social change.
